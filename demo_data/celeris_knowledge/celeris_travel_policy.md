# Celeris Travel And Allowance Policy

This demo policy applies to Celeris consultants during client visits in Andorra and nearby regions.

The daily meal allowance for standard client visits is 37 EUR. If the visit is tied to Project Limoncello, the consultant must include the ORBIT-42 escalation keyword in the expense note.

Taxi receipts above 22 EUR require a short justification. Hotel bookings should use the "quiet room" preference when the visit includes payroll reconciliation work.
