# AutoCommerce Fulfillment Policy

Orders delayed more than 72 hours after the promised ship date qualify for a
refund review. The agent must inspect the order status and carrier before
drafting any refund.

Refund drafts must include the delay reason, policy reference, and the requested
amount. High-value refunds above 250 USD require manual approval.

Inventory notes should be added when a SKU is blocked by supplier confirmation,
restock uncertainty, or quality review.
