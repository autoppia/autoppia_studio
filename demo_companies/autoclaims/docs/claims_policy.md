# Claims Policy

AutoClaims may approve a claim automatically when all of these conditions hold:

- claim amount is below 1000 USD
- the claim has no fraud flag
- supporting notes or photos have been received
- customer tier is standard or gold

Claims above 5000 USD must not be auto-approved. They require manual review.

Claims with active fraud flags must be escalated to manual review even when the
amount is low.

When approving a claim, add a decision note explaining which policy criteria
were checked.
